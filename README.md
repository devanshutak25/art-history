# Art Movements Timeline

An interactive, single-page timeline of art movements from **~40,000 BCE to the present**.

## What's Inside

A self-contained HTML page (`art-movements-timeline.html`) covering **9 eras** and dozens of movements:

- Prehistoric & Ancient Non-Western
- Classical Mediterranean & Asian
- Medieval European
- Renaissance & Early Modern
- 18th–19th Century
- Early 20th Century Avant-Garde
- Mid-20th Century
- Late 20th Century
- Contemporary (21st Century)

Each movement includes origin context, a description, key artists (linked to Wikipedia), and links to museums/resources.

## Features

- **Search** — filter movements by name, artist, or concept
- **Era filters** — toggle visibility by historical period
- **Expand/collapse** — view details for individual movements or all at once
- **Dark theme** — designed for comfortable reading

## Usage

Open `art-movements-timeline.html` in any modern browser. No build step, no dependencies.
